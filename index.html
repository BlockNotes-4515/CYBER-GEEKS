Assembling by CYBER GEEKS

Our project focuses on developing a Moon Mission Land Rover and a Robotic Monitoring Car for advanced exploration and monitoring tasks. The Moon Mission Land Rover is engineered to traverse the lunar surface, featuring robust wheels and adaptive suspension for optimal performance on rocky terrain. It is equipped with sensors for collecting vital environmental data such as temperature, radiation, and atmospheric composition, enabling autonomous navigation with real-time decision-making based on sensor inputs. In parallel, the Robotic Monitoring Car is designed for real-time surveillance and monitoring, equipped with cameras and sensors for live feedback and data transmission, and can be remotely operated to explore hazardous areas. The software for both vehicles is developed using C/C++ for efficient hardware control and Python for data processing and user interface development. Meticulous equipment assembling ensures precision engineering and integration testing, guaranteeing reliability and functionality in the harsh conditions of space exploration.
